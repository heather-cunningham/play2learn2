About

This project is for my enrollment in North Shore Community College's (Danvers, MA) Full Stack Developer Certificate program. While the math and literacy games on this site are actual functioning games (or yet to be developed ones), they have not been developed by a curriculum designer, teacher, academic, or professor. All claims to their educational efficacy are made-up data (by me) to populate this website project with content.

Disclaimer

The purpose of this website is for me to practice coding across the entire tech stack -- to practice my full stack web development skills. While you or your child/children may play the games on this site, no claim to their educational efficacy should be taken seriously.
