Math Facts

This game is for practicing arithmetic operations:

Addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division.

  1. Select an arithmetic operation to practice.
  2. Press "Go!"
  3. How many problems can you solve in 30 seconds?
**NOTE! If selecting Division, decimal point answers should be rounded to the nearest 100ths place at max (i.e., two digits past the decimal point maximum).
